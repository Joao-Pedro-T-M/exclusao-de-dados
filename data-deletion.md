# Flup3 - Data Deletion Request

**Last updated:** 06/24/2026

This page explains how users can request the deletion of data related to the Flup3 application, developed by João Pedro Torres Machado.

---

## How to Request Data Deletion

To request the deletion of data related to Flup3, please contact:

**Email:** jptorresmachado@gmail.com

Please include information that helps identify your request. Additional information may be requested to verify your identity before processing the request.

---

## Data That May Be Deleted

The following data may be deleted upon request when applicable:

* support-related communications;
* application-related data associated with the request;
* other information that can reasonably be linked to the user and is under the developer's control.

---

## Data That May Be Retained

Certain information may be retained when required for:

* legal obligations;
* fraud prevention and security purposes;
* accounting or tax requirements related to in-app purchases;
* technical and operational requirements.

Anonymous or aggregated information that cannot identify an individual user may also be retained.

---

## Retention Period

Some information may be retained for a limited period when required by law, regulation, security, fraud prevention, accounting, or technical requirements. After that period, the information will be deleted or anonymized when applicable.
